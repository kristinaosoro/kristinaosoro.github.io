COVID 19
DATA EXPLORATION

As a data analyst, I've dedicated myself to mastering the intricacies of data cleaning, transformation, and visualization. My goal is to showcase my analytical prowess through a deep dive into COVID-19 data. The pandemic has reshaped our world dramatically, and I aim to explore its global impact, focusing on death percentages, vaccination rates, and identifying the regions most significantly affected.

I sourced my dataset from 'Our World in Data: Coronavirus (COVID-19) Deaths' using BigQuery to clean, transform, and subsequently exported it into an Excel spreadsheet for integration into Tableau.

Here is the Tableau Story I crafted for COVID-19 between years of 2019 through 2023. The first slide dissects global COVID cases versus deaths, offering a comprehensive visualization. On the second slide, I delve into the comparison between the global population and the vaccinated population, providing a clear graphical representation.
